Beat the February Blues with our Famous Salmon Cookies!

Made using Washington's local Salmon, baked and grounded to perfection, mixed with cake flour, these salmon cookies are perfectly soft yet crisp around the edges to make you feel pampered at home!

Our Story - How it all began!

My grandfather Pat Sr. challenged my grandmother Patricia to make something sweet with salmon since it was so abundant in Washinton. Patricia took up the challenge and surprised my grandfather with the first batch of Pat's Salmon Cookies! Needless to say that my grandfather Pat Sr. loved it and told her to start selling the cookies in the neighborhood. Through word of mouth it became very popular and that is how Pat's Cookie Shop was born in 1938.All these years it has grown in popularity and has become a traditional symbol of Seattle. Today we have many more stores.
